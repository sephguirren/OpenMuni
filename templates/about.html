About OpenMuni

Bridging the gap between the local government and the citizens through digital transparency, real-time tracking, and open communication.

Our Mission

To provide every citizen with accessible, accurate, and up-to-date information regarding municipal projects and budget allocations, ensuring that public funds are utilized effectively for community development.

Our Vision

A municipality where governance is entirely transparent, citizens are actively engaged in proposing infrastructure improvements, and every public project is tracked from proposal to completion.

Core Pillars of the System

Financial Transparency

Citizens can view the exact budget allocated and spent for every infrastructure project.

24/7 AI Assistance

An intelligent chatbot is available around the clock to answer specific questions about project statuses and timelines.

Civic Participation

Empowering barangay officials and citizens to propose necessary projects directly to the municipal council.
