Transparency

This Transparency Portal is an informative tool developed by the Municipal Government to empower you, the citizen, by promoting honesty and accountability in how we deliver our services.

  • Accountability: Making sure public officials answer directly to the people they serve.
  • Participation: Creating clear ways for citizens to join in the development process and have a voice in local institutions.
  • Predictability: Having fair, clear rules that are applied consistently and equally to everyone.
  • Transparency: Keeping government information completely open and easily available to the general public.


GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

  • • Build Complete Trust: Use secure technology to lock in project details and budgets, so no one can secretly change them after they are approved.
  • • Make Information Easy to Find: Use a friendly AI chatbot to help anyone quickly find the project updates they need.
  • • Improve Government Work: Make it faster and safer for local leaders and barangay officials to propose, approve, and track community projects.
  • • Encourage Citizen Involvement: Give ordinary people the power to easily check and verify government records themselves, so they feel more involved in their barangay.
